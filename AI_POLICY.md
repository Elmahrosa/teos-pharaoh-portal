# TEOS Egypt — AI Governance Policy

## Purpose
This policy governs all **AI agents, assistants, and automated systems**
operating within the TEOS Egypt ecosystem.

AI is a **tool**, not an authority.

---

## Permitted AI Functions
- Data analysis and reporting
- Governance proposal assistance
- Customer and civic support
- Fraud detection and monitoring
- Content generation (non-binding)

---

## Prohibited AI Actions
- Autonomous treasury movement
- Final governance decisions
- Identity or KYC approval
- Legal or regulatory determinations
- Undisclosed influence on voting

---

## Human-in-the-Loop Requirement
- All critical actions require human approval
- AI outputs must be explainable
- Logs are mandatory and auditable

---

## Transparency
- AI-generated content must be labeled
- Decision rationale must be retained
- Users may opt out of AI interactions

---

## Ethical Alignment
- No manipulation or dark patterns
- No surveillance beyond necessity
- Respect for cultural and civic values

---

## Incident Response
- Immediate suspension of misbehaving agents
- Public disclosure of AI incidents
- Post-incident governance review

---

**AI serves the civic mission —  
the civic mission never serves AI.**
