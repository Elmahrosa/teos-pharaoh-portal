# TEOS Egypt — Civic Blockchain Constitution

## Preamble
We, the builders of TEOS Egypt, establish this Constitution to encode
**trust, transparency, and civic dignity** into digital infrastructure.

Technology shall serve people.
Infrastructure shall outlive speculation.
Governance shall be visible, auditable, and fair.

---

## Article I — Civic Purpose
TEOS Egypt exists to:
- Enable digital citizenship
- Provide transparent governance
- Expand financial and civic inclusion
- Preserve cultural and national identity

---

## Article II — Sovereignty
- Users are **citizens**, not products
- Data belongs to its originators
- Governance is participatory by design

---

## Article III — Infrastructure Principles
- Open by default
- Secure by design
- Compliant by architecture
- Decentralized progressively

---

## Article IV — Governance
- Authority derives from contribution and accountability
- No permanent centralized control
- Emergency powers are temporary and reviewable

---

## Article V — Treasury
- Funds serve civic development
- All movements are auditable
- Misuse constitutes constitutional violation

---

## Article VI — AI Systems
- AI may assist, never dominate
- AI decisions must be explainable
- Human oversight is mandatory

---

## Article VII — Amendments
- Amendments require supermajority approval
- Public review period mandatory
- No amendment may violate civic-first principles

---

**This Constitution is a living document,  
binding technology to humanity, not the reverse.**
