TEOS Pharaoh Portal is Egypt’s **sovereign digital gateway**: one place where citizens, businesses, and government can securely interact across **finance, identity, mobility, and governance**.

It is the user-facing entry point into the broader TEOS stack: **Bankchain, CivicChain, Pi Smart City, PiTaxi, and compliance / AI auditing services**.

---

## What is TEOS Pharaoh Portal?

A **web and mobile portal** that unifies:

- **Digital identity**  
  Secure, reusable IDs for people, businesses, and devices.

- **Banking & settlement (TEOS Bankchain)**  
  Rails for payments, remittances, and on-chain financial services.

- **Smart city & mobility (Pi Smart City, PiTaxi)**  
  Transport, urban services, and IoT-powered infrastructure.

- **Civic services & governance (CivicChain, AI Auditor)**  
  Licensing, permits, registries, audits, and policy enforcement.

All of this is exposed behind a **single login and consistent UX**.

---

## Who is it for?

- **Citizens & residents**  
  Access banking, services, and mobility from one trusted portal.

- **Businesses & banks**  
  Integrate with sovereign rails for KYC, payments, and trade.

- **Government & regulators**  
  Operate and supervise digital services with **on-chain auditability**.

- **Developers & ecosystem partners**  
  Build apps and services on top of TEOS rails and APIs.

---

## Core Principles

- **Sovereign-by-design**  
  Built for Egypt’s legal, regulatory, and cultural context.

- **Composability over silos**  
  Identity, payments, and services are **modular** but interoperable.

- **User-centric security**  
  Strong identity, clear consent flows, and transparent audit trails.

- **Global reach**  
  Positions Egypt as a **Web3 and digital trade hub** across GCC, Africa, and Europe.
